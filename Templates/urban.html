Urban Flood Awareness Tips

1. Avoid Littering
Garbage can clog city drainage systems. Dispose of waste properly to reduce flood risks.
2. Report Blocked Drains
Notify your municipality about clogged stormwater drains or overflowing gutters.
3. Elevate Electrical Systems
Keep electrical components above expected flood levels to avoid short-circuits and danger.
4. Secure Basement Areas
Use sump pumps and seal basement walls to prevent water infiltration during heavy rainfall.
5. Park Safely
Avoid leaving vehicles on low-lying roads that could become submerged quickly during storms.
6. Install Backflow Valves
Prevent sewage from backing up into your home during floods by using proper plumbing fixtures.
7. Monitor Urban Alerts
Stay tuned to local weather and emergency services for real-time urban flood alerts.
